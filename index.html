Our Story

Inspired by the iconic Spanish film Jamón Jamón starring Penélope Cruz and Javier Bardem, our restaurant embodies the same passion, drama, and celebration of life that made the film unforgettable.

Founded by Argentinian restaurateur Sergio Bevilacqua, who brings over three decades of gastronomic excellence, Jamon Jamon is where Mediterranean sophistication meets Italian warmth, with a passionate Spanish heart.

Under the expert guidance of Chef Manuel Naula, we create dishes that honor tradition while embracing innovation. Our focus on high-quality pork dishes, Spanish tapas, and Italian classics makes us a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
